hello!
My name is Mahira /ma-hee-rah/ and I was born and raised on the bustling streets of Cairo, Egypt. I currently live in California, studying Architecture at UC Berkeley. My passion for design leaves me in awe at the aesthetics of everything around me. I'm fascinated by the impact that design decisions have on the human mind and how they can influence our everyday experience of the world. I spend most of my free time working on front end web development projects, browsing spotify playlists, taking photographs, and finding every excuse that I can to travel and explore new places around the world.
