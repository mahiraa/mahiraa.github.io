Tarishi Jain
In July 2016, ISAB lost a dear and valued member, Tarishi Jain, whose life was taken in the Dhaka terrorist attack in Bangladesh. Being in ISAB core for her entire freshman year, Tarishi has left a legacy behind in ISAB and thus, she will never be forgotten. If you would like to make a submission to this page, please email mahira@berkeley.edu.

Rest in peace, Tarishi. You will be missed.
